SECTION 4 — LEGAL & COMPANY STRUCTURE (Don't ignore this)
================================================================================

  ❌  4.1  Company incorporated
           → You need a legal entity before taking any investment
           → NZ: Register on the Companies Office (NZ Companies Register)
           → Straightforward, ~$130 NZD, takes 1 day
           → Do this BEFORE any funding conversation gets serious

  ❌  4.2  Shareholding structure
           → Who owns what? Even if it's 100% you right now — write it down
           → Investors will ask. Have a clean cap table from day one.

  ❌  4.3  IP ownership confirmation
           → All code, brand, content must be owned by the company (not you personally)
           → Assign IP to company when you incorporate
           → Get advice from a startup lawyer (many NZ lawyers offer free first consult)

  ❌  4.4  Privacy policy
           → You are storing personal data (names, locations, saved items)
           → NZ Privacy Act 2020 applies
           → You need a basic privacy policy on the app — even a simple one
           → Template available from NZ Privacy Commissioner website
           → DIFFERENTIATOR TO HIGHLIGHT: Odin's Save Inheritance model
             strips original identity beyond 1 hop. The adder's name never
             travels to people they don't know. This is MORE privacy-protective
             than almost any social platform. Make this a feature in the policy,
             not just a legal clause.
           → Draft user-facing explanation already written in project status doc.
             Use that as the basis for the privacy policy plain-language section.

  ❌  4.5  Terms of service / user agreement
           → What users agree to when they join
           → Protects you legally if something goes wrong
           → Can use a template to start — customise for your use case
           → Key clause to include: visibility consent — user acknowledges that
             items set to "Friends" can be saved and travel one hop further
             anonymously. The in-app tooltip already covers this at add-time.

  ❌  4.6  Founder agreement (if co-founders added later)
           → Not needed now (solo), but critical before adding a co-founder
           → Covers: vesting, IP assignment, role split, exit provisions
