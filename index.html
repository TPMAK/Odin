How it works

Odin turns your circle's discoveries into a library you can search anytime — no algorithms, just people you trust.

01 Search like you'd ask a friend. "Good coffee near work" — not just keywords. Odin searches what your circle has saved.
02 Save the little discoveries from real life. Places, products, services — the small things worth remembering, all in one place.
03 Works across languages. Your circle may speak different languages — Odin understands them all.
04 Private by default. Only friends you approve can see your saves. Your circle, your rules.
