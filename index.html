vouch

See the world through your friends' eyes

Vouch is an AI-powered app that helps you discover what your friends actually love—places, products, books, online creators, advice, anything.

No more googling strangers' opinions. Just search naturally and find what your trusted circle adds.

💬

AI Semantic Search

Ask questions like you're texting a friend. "Good coffee for studying?" The AI understands what you mean, not just exact keywords—like having a conversation with all your friends at once.

🔒

Private by Default

Like a private group chat, not social media. Only your friends see what you share. No strangers, no companies mining your data to sell you stuff.

🌍

Anything & Everything

Not just cafes and restaurants. Your friend's favourite YouTuber, the book that changed their life, the dentist who doesn't hurt, the best budget laptop—if your friend loves it, you can find it.

👥

Your Friend Network

Stop asking the same question in 5 different group chats. Your friends have already answered—you just didn't see it. Like having all your friends' recommendations in one searchable place.

Why this matters

Every place you add, everything you save—you're planting seeds that help you and your friends discover something meaningful. Your contributions aren't just data points. They're connections. They bring people closer, no matter the distance.

You're not just testing an app. You're helping build a community where trust and genuine experiences matter more than algorithms and ads.
